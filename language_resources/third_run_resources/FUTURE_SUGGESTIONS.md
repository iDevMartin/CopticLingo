#### 3.2 Milestone Proficiency Tests

**Impact**: Validates cumulative learning at key checkpoints

- Reading comprehension (Gospel passage)

- Grammar analysis (Identify parts of speech in complex sentence)

**Certification:**

- Shareable badges for social media
